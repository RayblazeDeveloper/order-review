Methodologies Developed or Omitted:

Although the Income Capitalization Approach may be relevant to the subject property, it has been omitted for this analysis. The Income Capitalization Approach would be considered a secondary supporting analysis and its omission does not have a material effect on the resulting value of the subject property. The cost approach is typically employed on new or nearly new improvements due to the difficulty in estimating accrued depreciation. Furthermore, it is not typically utilized by market participants in buy/sell decisions. Based on the above, the Cost Approach was not considered relevant. The agreed upon Scope of Work is to rely on the Sales Comparison Approach only which will provide a credible result.
